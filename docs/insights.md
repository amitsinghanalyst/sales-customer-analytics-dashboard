# 📊 Key Business Insights

---

## 1. Revenue Concentration

- The **Bikes category contributes ~96% of total revenue**, indicating strong dependency on a single product category.
- This suggests potential risk and opportunity for diversification.

---

## 2. Sales Trend Growth

- Sales show a **consistent upward trend from 2012 to 2013**.
- Indicates business growth and increasing demand over time.

---

## 3. Pareto Effect (80/20 Rule)

- A small number of products (Top 10) generate a **majority of total revenue**.
- Highlights importance of focusing on high-performing products.

---

## 4. Customer Segmentation Insight

- Majority of customers fall under the **“New” segment (~79%)**.
- Indicates strong acquisition but potential gap in retention.

---

## 5. Product Performance Distribution

- High-performing products generate **significantly higher revenue with moderate quantity**.
- Suggests premium pricing or high-value products.

---

## 6. Low-Performing Products

- Bottom products contribute **minimal revenue**.
- These products may require:
  - Pricing adjustments  
  - Marketing efforts  
  - Or discontinuation  

---

## 7. Geographic Distribution

- The **United States has the highest customer base**, followed by Australia and the UK.
- Indicates key markets driving business growth.

---

## 8. Business Opportunity

- Improve **customer retention strategies**
- Expand **non-bike product categories**
- Focus marketing on **high-value products**

---

## 📌 Summary

The business is:
- Strongly driven by a single category (Bikes)
- Experiencing steady growth
- Dependent on a small set of high-performing products

Strategic focus should be on **diversification, retention, and optimization**.
